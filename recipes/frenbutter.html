Fren Butter

two simple ingredients are needed: fren herbs & butter

Gently break apart the frenship buds by hand into popcorn-sized pieces and place them into a mason jar. Do not grind. Gently put the lid on the mason jar and place the jar in the oven for decarboxylation bake at 240° F for 40 minutes

divide frenship herbs up into desired jars and fill with butter. let it cook in a water bath for around 4 hours

strain, mold into shapes, enjoy
