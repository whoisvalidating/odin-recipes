420 Fren brownies

  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ cup fren butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Optional toppings nuts, chocolate chips, caramel, or candy

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Grease an 8"x8" metal baking pan and set it aside.
  2. In a medium-sized bowl, mix the dry ingredients. Sift the flour and the cocoa powder to remove any lumps, then add the salt, followed by the baking powder. Set aside.
  3. Cream the fren butter, granulated white, and brown sugar in a large bowl with a hand mixer.
  4. Add the eggs and vanilla to the creamed sugar.
  5. Beat on medium speed for one minute (the mixture's color should lighten a bit).
  6. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients.
  7. Beat on low speed until everything is well incorporated.
  8. Transfer the batter to the reserved greased baking pan. Lightly tap the pan to even out the batter.
  9. If you want to add any additional toppings, add them now.
  10. Place the pan in the oven to bake for 25-30 minutes. The time will depend on your oven and your brownie consistency preferences. Test the finished brownies with a toothpick*
  11. If you can wait, allow the pan of brownies to cool for 30 minutes on a wire rack before cutting them into squares and removing them from the pan.
  12. Enjoy these easy fren brownies over ice cream, in a Sundae, topped with dark chocolate chips - and repeat!
