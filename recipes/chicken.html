cranberry maple chicken

sweet and juicy breasts

everyone likes breasts, these ones are covered in maple and sugar. These are... 4 DA BOIZ

    What You Will Need?

  • 2 cups frozen or fresh cranberries
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 6 boneless skinless chicken breasts (halved)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup

    How It Will Go Down!

  1. in a saucepan, combine cranberries, water and sugar. cook over medium heat untill berries pop (15is min)
  2. while thats going on, sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper. cook in a large nonstick skillet in the oil till juices run clear (5-6 min a side)
  3. stir the maple syrup in the cranberry sauce and spoon over chicken. Enjoy!
