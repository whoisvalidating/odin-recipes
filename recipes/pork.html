    What You Will Need?

  • 4 boneless pork loin chops
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary (crush that shit)
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme (crush that shit)
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

    How It Will Go Down!
